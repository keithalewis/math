Algebras of Sets

Keith A. Lewis

May 30, 2026

If S is a set then an algebra of sets on S is a collection of subsets of S that is closed under complement and union.

Exercise. If A and B are in an algebra, then so is their intersection.

Hint: De Morgan’s laws.

If \mathcal{A} is an algebra of sets on S and s\in S let [s] = \cap\{A\in\mathcal{A}\mid s\in A\} be the intersecton of all sets in the algebra that contain s. Define [\mathcal{A}] = \{[s]\mid s\in S\}.
