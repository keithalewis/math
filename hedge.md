---
title: Hedging
author: Keith A. Lewis
---

A derivative is a contract: "I will give you this on these dates if you will give me that on those dates."

A cash settled derivative is defined by a finite number of increasing stopping times
$\hat{\tau_j}$ and amounts $\hat{A}_j$. A buyer pays some amount up front to the
seller who is contractually obligated to make the specified payments at the specified dates.
